PRO TECHNICAL SERVICES — IMAGE GUIDE
======================================

The logo is already included (logo.png), extracted from your uploaded
business card. Add your own photos for the rest using the exact
filenames below, dropped into this /gallery folder.

  hero-car.jpg          — a hero vehicle shot (the Yaris from your card,
                          or any hybrid vehicle you service). Works best
                          as a vehicle on a transparent or dark background.
  texture-leather.jpg    — optional dark leather/textured background
                          (matches the card's black textured backdrop).
                          If omitted, the hero still works fine without it.
  about-workshop.jpg     — a photo of your workshop, bay, or team at work
                          (portrait orientation, roughly 4:5)

TIP: hero-car.jpg has the biggest visual impact — prioritize that one.
If you'd like, you can also just reuse the original car photo from your
uploaded business card design.
