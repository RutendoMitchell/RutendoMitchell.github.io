About Us

Six chairs, and a rule about really listening first.

Marlow & Thread opened in 2019 with one idea: a salon doesn't need forty stylists to be great — it needs the right six.

Our Story

Started by a stylist tired of ten-minute consultations.

Founder Iris Calloway spent eight years cutting hair in salons built for volume — fast turnovers, rushed consultations, the same three cuts repeated all day. Marlow & Thread is the opposite bet: fewer chairs, longer appointments, and a stylist who actually remembers what you said last time.

We named it after the street the original shop sat on, and the idea that good hair, like good thread, is about consistency — the same care, every single visit.
