About Us

One hearth.
No shortcuts.

Ember & Salt opened with a single rule: if it can't be cooked over wood, it doesn't go on the menu.

Chef Marisol Ferro spent six years cooking over open flame in Oaxaca and Basque coastal kitchens before bringing that discipline home. Our hearth burns oak and applewood from local orchards, and every dish — from the bread to the dessert — passes through it. The menu changes with what the fire and the season allow.

We seat 38 guests across the main room and a private hearth-side counter, where you can watch the kitchen work the coals.

What We Offer

From the hearth to your table, a few ways to eat with us.

01 Dinner Service A rotating small-plates menu built daily around the fire and the morning market run. Walk-ins welcome at the hearth-side counter.
02 Chef's Counter Six seats facing the hearth for a multi-course tasting menu, narrated course by course by whoever is working the coals that night.
03 Private Hearth Room A 12-seat private room with its own small fire pit, for dinners, tastings, and small gatherings. Custom menus on request.
04 Catering & Live-Fire Events We bring a portable hearth off-site for private events — full-service catering with live-fire cooking on location.
05 Wine & Pairings A small, low-intervention wine list chosen to stand up to smoke and char, with optional pairings for any tasting menu.
